COOKIE

Cookie is a method to store data on the client machine (browser).
Cookies store a limited amount of data.

Types of Cookies

1. Non-Persistent Cookie
2. Persistent Cookie

Non-Persistent Cookie

It is a cookie which is automatically destroyed after closing the browser window.

Persistent Cookie

It is a cookie which is not destroyed after closing the browser because we set a time limit (e.g., 24 hours).
